Farming Equipment - Ripper

What is a Ripper?

A ripper is a powerful agricultural implement used for deep tillage to break up compacted soil layers, improve drainage, and enhance root penetration. It is commonly attached to a tractor and consists of strong steel shanks designed to penetrate hard soil.

Uses of a Ripper

  • Soil Aeration: Enhances oxygen circulation in the soil.
  • Breaking Hardpan Layers: Loosens compacted soil for better root growth.
  • Improving Water Infiltration: Prevents waterlogging and enhances soil moisture retention.
  • Preparing Land for Planting: Makes the soil more suitable for seeding and crop growth.

Advantages of Using a Ripper

  • ✅ Reduces soil erosion
  • ✅ Increases crop yield by improving root access to nutrients
  • ✅ Enhances drainage and water retention
